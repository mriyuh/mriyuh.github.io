Our Story

Mriyuh Studio was founded by Mriyuh — an artist, a designer, and a dreamer with a sewing machine.

Rooted in the heritage of Britain’s traditional mill industry and shaped by the quiet strength of Eastern ethics, she believes the union of these two worlds forms the foundation of her studio: precision and play, discipline and daring.

Our Values

Longevity

Built to last — in both design and quality. We don’t chase trends, and we don’t make pieces meant to be replaced.

Sustainability

Our production is optimised for the planet — because we have no interest in trashing our only home.

Aesthetic

Beauty is not a luxury — it’s nourishment for the soul. We create what truly satisfies.

Care

From the drawing board to your doorstep, we promise to give our full attention — always.

Discipline

Every detail is intentional. We honor tradition through craftsmanship and ethical choices.

Our Mission

Art has always been a platform to promote counterculture — to challenge societal norms and traditional values. But in a time when norms and values seem to have disappeared altogether, true counterculture is about bringing them back.

At Mriyuh Studio, we use story, design, and imagination to reintroduce what the world has lost: depth, discipline, harmony, and care.

Through our productions and designs, we aim to cultivate a beauty that endures — grounded in sustainability, crafted with quality, and built to outlast passing trends.
