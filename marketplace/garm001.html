Product Story

This tee represents the beginning of Mriyuh Studio’s fashion journey — a celebration of material honesty, slow design, and hands-on craftsmanship. It was imagined not in a corporate office, but at a humble workbench, where fabric and leather met story and soul.

Care Instructions

  • Machine wash cold with like colors.
  • Use mild detergent. Avoid bleach.
  • Do not tumble dry — hang dry for best results.
  • Iron inside-out on low if needed.
  • Note: Remove the leather tag before washing or drying. It is not machine-safe.
